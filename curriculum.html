The HIAIS Program

Balanced Islamic knowledge is among the most essential necessities for every Muslim. It guides hearts, strengthens worship, and brings clarity to life.

Qur’an on the people of knowledge

{Allah bears witness that Laa ilaaha illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He), and the angels, and those having knowledge (also give this witness); (He is always) maintaining His creation in Justice.}
Surat Aal-Imran: 18
{And say, ‘My Lord, increase me in knowledge.’}

Knowledge here is not decoration. It is Shari‘ah knowledge that corrects belief, worship, and action.

Message from Us

Our aim is to present authentic Islamic knowledge based on the understanding of the best generations and to make it accessible through effective, student-centered teaching.

We combine sincerity, sound methodology, and simple tools to deliver lessons clearly, while keeping precision in language and Shari‘ah.

Objectives

  • Disseminate balanced Islamic knowledge to the widest audience.
  • Serve beginners and advanced students through a progressive curriculum.
  • Clarify the methodology of the best generations with evidence and clarity.
  • Make learning accessible in Aqeedah, Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, and manners.
  • Promote distance learning with professionalism in Arabic and Islamic Studies.
